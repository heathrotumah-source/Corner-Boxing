Defense
↙
Slip inside / outside
Head moves to one side of their punch. Counter immediately.
↓
Roll / bob under
Knees bend under the hook. Come up on the opposite side.
←
Pull / lean back
Upper body leans away. Weight to rear foot.
🤚
Parry
Open hand redirects their punch offline. Counter immediately.
🛡
Block / cover up
Gloves high, elbows tight. Absorb on the arms. Temporary.
⬇
Duck
Straight down with the legs. Head below their punch.
Footwork
→
Step in / Step back
Lead foot first to advance. Rear foot first to retreat.
↗
Angle step / Pivot
Step off the centre line 45°. Punch from the new angle.
